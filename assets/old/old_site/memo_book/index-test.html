Sample Works

Dow Jones & the Wall Street JournalSummer 2013

As a member of the Dow Jones team, I was added to the blogs team as part of the Wall Street Journal. I worked with a small team of 5 to help oversee the 43 online blogs that the Wall Street Journal has to offer. I created one of their newest blogs, The Experts this past summer using PHP and the Wordpress platform. In addition, I implemented a re-design to the Digits blog this past summer. In addition to those blogs, I created PHP functions that allowed for a smoother process of sending stories to the Wall Street Journal's main downstream feed for the blogs and main Online Wall Street Journal articles. I worked in a real-life agile environment with multiple sprint releases and experts in the fields.

TechChangeSummer 2012

During the summer of 2012, I interned at the Washington D.C. based startup company, TechChange as a web developer intern. The company provides online non-credit classes on recent topics involving technology in the world. Seminars are purely online, and lectures are also. I helped provide back-end support to the main site using the Wordpress platform, CSS, HTML, and PHP. I helped to provide functions allowing fluid back-end to front-end support for classes and online presentations.

FCAatUMDSeptebmer 2011 - December 2012

As part of the Filipino Cultural Association at the University of Maryland, I served as the Female Freshman Representative and also as the Webmaster. I helped to create the jQeury effects for the Semi-formal 2011 website, I also created the FilAmplified website for the event in Octover 2012, and I managed the main FCAatUMD website using HTML and CSS.In addition, I created multiple videos using Final Cut Pro X and Adobe Premier Pro on the FCAatUMD Youtube account

University of Maryland - Division of Student AffairsSeptember 2013 - Present

As part of the Division of Student Affairs at the University of Maryland, I help manage the main website. Using HTML and CSS, I have created multiple scholarship websites as well as creating banners for those websites using Photoshop. In addition, I am in the process of placing the website onto the Drupal content management system.
